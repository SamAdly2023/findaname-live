Tips for Choosing a Username

  • Keep it short: Aim for 15 characters or less for easy memorization.
  • Avoid numbers: Unless they're part of your brand, numbers look unprofessional.
  • Check availability: Secure your username across all platforms, even if you don't plan to use them yet.
  • Be consistent: Use the exact same handle everywhere when possible.
