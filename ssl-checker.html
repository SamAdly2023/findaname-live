Understanding SSL Certificates

SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) certificates are essential for website security. They encrypt data transmitted between a user's browser and your web server, protecting sensitive information like passwords and credit card numbers.

Certificate Types

  • Domain Validated (DV): Basic encryption, verifies domain ownership only. Quick to obtain.
  • Organization Validated (OV): Verifies organization identity. Shows company name in certificate details.
  • Extended Validation (EV): Highest level of trust. Requires thorough vetting of the organization.
  • Wildcard: Covers a domain and all its subdomains (*.example.com).
