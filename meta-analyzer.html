Essential Meta Tags for SEO

Meta tags provide metadata about your webpage to search engines and social media platforms. Proper meta tag optimization is crucial for SEO and social sharing.

Must-Have Meta Tags

Title Tag

The most important on-page SEO element. Keep it under 60 characters. Include your target keyword near the beginning.

Meta Description

Summarizes page content for search results. Keep it under 160 characters. Include a call-to-action.

Open Graph Tags

Control how your page appears on Facebook, LinkedIn. Essential tags: og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url.

Twitter Cards

Optimize appearance on Twitter. Use twitter:card, twitter:title, twitter:description, twitter:image.

Best Practices

  • Unique Tags: Every page should have unique title and description tags.
  • Keyword Placement: Include primary keywords naturally in title and description.
  • Image Optimization: Use high-quality images (1200x630px) for OG and Twitter cards.
  • Canonical URL: Use rel="canonical" to prevent duplicate content issues.
